Visit to the National Space Centre

A great night was had by MTU staff and students at the National Space Centre Elfordstown Earthstation last night.

Three speakers from the space industry in Europe shared fascinating insights into their own careers as well as the opportunities available to students who are interested in the sector. Many thanks to Bruce Hannah, Ben Smith and Jon Green for their insights and advice, and to Rory Fitzpatrick and everyone at the NSC for organizing this event!
